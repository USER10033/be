Our core services
Delivering Powerful Logistics Solutions
We give you more control and greater value across your supply chain.
Sea Freight
We provide a reliable and cost-effective service for international sea transportation. Leveraging the best and biggest ocean carriers, we offer full container load (FCL) as well as less-than container load (LCL) freight management to and from major ports across the globe.
Air Freight
A fast and effective way of transporting goods globally while balancing freight costs and transit time. With direct connections to major international airlines, we offer flexible and economical air freight solutions.
Road Freight
Through our close relationship with hundreds of carriers and partners, our road freight service offers a wide and reliable network. From full truck loads (FTL) to less-than truck loads (LTL) to groupage deliveries, we deliver nationally and throughout Europe.
Rail Freight
Rail freight is an increasingly popular solution for the movement of goods between China and Europe. At Primeplusexpresswe provide a punctual and time efficient rail freight service.
Amazon FBA
Primeplusexpressare Amazon Service Provider Network (SPN) certified partners. With our expertise and knowledge in Amazon FBA requirements, we offer a full range of tailor-made logistics services to Amazon FBA sellers globally.
Warehousing & Distribution
With strategically positioned warehouse locations, we offer container handling, long and short-term storage, added-value warehousing services and inventory distribution – both domestically and internationally.
Customs Clearance
Our comprehensive customs clearance services assist both import and export shipments arriving and departing from UK ports and airports. We navigate UK customs smoothly and efficiently and to the highest professional standards.
Cargo Insurance
We work with selected partners to offer industry-specific insurance. Protecting your company from financial loss resulting from loss or damage to your shipments while in transit anywhere in the world by road, rail, sea or air.
